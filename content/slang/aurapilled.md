# What Does "Aurapilled" Mean on TikTok?

**"Aurapilled"** is a TikTok slang term used to describe someone who has been exposed to or converted to a new way of thinking about personal energy and confidence. It combines the concept of "aura" (personal energy) with the suffix "-pilled" (from "redpilled," meaning awakened to a new perspective).

## Definition

> **Aurapilled**: Having been exposed to or converted to a new way of thinking about personal energy and confidence.
>
> *Part of speech:* **adjective**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "Since I started meditating, I've been aurapilled" | A person describing their transformation |
| "That video completely aurapilled me" | Someone describing a transformative experience |
| "Aurapilled people just hit different" | A comment on people who have embraced this mindset |

## Cultural Context

The term **"aurapilled"** reflects TikTok's fascination with self-improvement, spirituality, and personal energy. The "-pilled" suffix has become a popular way to describe being initiated into a new way of thinking, originating from "redpilled" (from The Matrix) and evolving into various compound terms.

## Origin

The term emerged from TikTok in the mid-2020s, combining "aura" with the "-pilled" suffix that originated from "redpilled" in online culture. It represents the platform's unique ability to blend spiritual concepts with internet slang.
