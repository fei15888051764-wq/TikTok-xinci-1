# What Does "That's AI" Mean on TikTok?

**"That's AI"** is a TikTok phrase used to describe something that seems too perfect, artificial, or generated by AI rather than being authentic. The term reflects growing awareness of AI-generated content.

## Definition

> **That's AI**: Used to describe something that seems too perfect, artificial, or generated by AI rather than being authentic.
>
> *Part of speech:* **phrase**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "That's AI, no way that's real" | Skepticism about authentic-looking content |
| "Her photos are giving 'that's AI'" | Commenting on overly perfect images |
| "That's AI behavior" | Describing something that seems automated |

## Cultural Context

The phrase **"that's AI"** reflects TikTok's response to the rise of AI-generated content. It represents both skepticism about artificial content and a humorous way to call out things that seem too perfect to be real.

## Related Slang

- **NPC Behavior** — Actions that seem scripted or automated
- **Bot** — An automated account or someone acting robotic
- **Catfish** — Someone who uses a fake identity online
- **Filtered** — Using beauty filters to alter appearance

/ðæts ˈeɪ ˈaɪ/

## Origin

The phrase emerged on TikTok as AI-generated content became more prevalent. It reflects the platform's role in discussing the impact of technology on authenticity.
