# What Does "Bed Rotting" Mean on TikTok?

**"Bed Rotting"** is a TikTok slang term that describes the act of spending excessive time in bed engaged in unproductive activities, typically scrolling on your phone or watching content. While often used humorously, the term also reflects conversations about mental health and self-care.

## Definition

> **Bed Rotting**: Spending excessive time in bed doing unproductive activities like scrolling on your phone.
>
> *Part of speech:* **verb / noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "I've been bed rotting all weekend" | A person admitting to spending all weekend in bed |
| "Bed rotting is my favorite hobby" | A humorous take on the activity |
| "Stop bed rotting and go outside" | A friend encouraging someone to be more active |

## Cultural Context

The term **"bed rotting"** emerged as a way for Gen Z and Millennials to discuss the balance between rest and productivity. While it can be a sign of depression or burnout when excessive, many users frame it as a form of self-care or a necessary break from the demands of daily life.

## Related Slang

- **Goblin Mode** — Unapologetically prioritizing one's own comfort and desires
- **Brain Rot** — The deterioration of mental faculties from consuming too much low-quality online content
- **Touch Grass** — A reminder to go outside and experience the real world
- **Doom Scrolling** — Compulsively scrolling through negative news or content

## Origin

The term gained popularity on TikTok in the early-to-mid 2020s as users began sharing their experiences of spending extended periods in bed. It reflects the generation's openness about mental health and the normalization of discussing previously stigmatized behaviors.
