# What Does "Solar Rage" Mean on TikTok?

**"Solar Rage"** is a TikTok slang term that describes extreme anger or frustration triggered by hot weather or excessive sun exposure. The term humorously attributes bad mood to the sun's effects.

## Definition

> **Solar Rage**: Extreme anger or frustration triggered by hot weather or excessive sun exposure.
>
> *Part of speech:* **noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "I've got major solar rage today" | Expressing frustration due to heat |
| "The solar rage is real" | Acknowledging heat-induced irritability |
| "Solar rage hitting different at noon" | Commenting on midday heat frustration |

## Cultural Context

The term **"solar rage"** reflects TikTok's tendency to create humorous terms for everyday experiences. It provides a way to discuss the genuine effects of weather on mood while maintaining a lighthearted tone.
