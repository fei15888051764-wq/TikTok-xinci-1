# What Does "Quiet Ambition" Mean on TikTok?

**"Quiet Ambition"** is a TikTok slang term that describes pursuing goals and success without broadcasting it on social media. It represents a shift away from the "hustle culture" of constantly sharing achievements.

## Definition

> **Quiet Ambition**: Pursuing goals and success without broadcasting it on social media; moving in silence.
>
> *Part of speech:* **noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "2025 is all about quiet ambition" | A resolution to focus on goals privately |
| "Quiet ambition hits different" | Appreciating the value of private success |
| "She has quiet ambition, you can see it" | Recognizing someone's focused drive |

## Cultural Context

The term **"quiet ambition"** reflects a cultural shift away from the performative aspect of social media. It encourages focusing on personal growth without the need for external validation.
