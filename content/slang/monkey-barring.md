# What Does "Monkey Barring" Mean on TikTok?

**"Monkey Barring"** is a TikTok slang term that describes moving from one relationship to another without taking time to be single, similar to how children swing from one bar to the next on monkey bars.

## Definition

> **Monkey Barring**: Moving from one relationship to another without taking time to be single, like swinging on monkey bars.
>
> *Part of speech:* **verb / noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "She's been monkey barring for years" | Describing someone who never stays single |
| "Monkey barring is exhausting" | Expressing fatigue from constant relationships |
| "Stop monkey barring and take time for yourself" | Advice to focus on self before dating |

## Cultural Context

The term **"monkey barring"** reflects TikTok's conversations about healthy relationships and the importance of taking time to be single. It provides a vivid metaphor for a common dating pattern.
