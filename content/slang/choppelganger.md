# What Does "Choppelganger" Mean on TikTok?

**"Choppelganger"** is a TikTok slang term that combines "chopped" (rejected/less attractive) with "doppelganger" (look-alike) to describe someone who looks exactly like another person but is considered a less attractive version.

## Definition

> **Choppelganger**: Someone who looks exactly like another person but is considered less attractive or "chopped" version.
>
> *Part of speech:* **noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "He's her choppelganger" | Describing someone who looks like another but less attractive |
| "I found my choppelganger" | A humorous way of saying you found a less attractive look-alike |
| "Choppelganger energy" | A caption on a video comparing two look-alikes |

## Cultural Context

The term **"choppelganger"** reflects TikTok's tendency to create compound words by blending existing slang terms. It represents the platform's creative approach to language and humor.

## Related Slang

- **Doppelganger** — A person who looks exactly like another
- **Chopped** — Rejected or considered less attractive
- **Twin** — Someone who looks very similar to another person
- **Clone** — An exact copy or replica

The term emerged on TikTok as a creative blend of "chopped" and "doppelganger," reflecting the platform's culture of wordplay and compound slang terms.
