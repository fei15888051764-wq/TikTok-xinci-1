# What Does "Glitch Walking" Mean on TikTok?

**"Glitch Walking"** is a TikTok slang term that describes walking in an awkward, stuttering manner that resembles a video game glitch. The term is often used humorously to describe someone who looks like they're experiencing a technical malfunction while walking.
