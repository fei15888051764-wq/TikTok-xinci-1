# What Does "Cortisol Coded" Mean on TikTok?

**"Cortisol Coded"** is a TikTok slang term that describes someone whose behavior is driven by chronic stress and anxiety, operating in a constant state of fight-or-flight. The term references cortisol, the body's primary stress hormone.

## Definition

> **Cortisol Coded**: Someone whose behavior is driven by stress and anxiety; operating in a constant state of fight-or-flight.
>
> *Part of speech:* **adjective**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "I've been cortisol coded since finals week" | A student describing their stress-driven behavior |
| "That job has her cortisol coded" | Describing someone stressed by their work |
| "Cortisol coded vibes only" | A humorous take on being constantly stressed |

## Cultural Context

The term **"cortisol coded"** reflects TikTok's trend of using scientific and medical terminology in slang. It speaks to Gen Z's awareness of mental health and the physiological effects of stress, while also providing a way to discuss these issues with humor.

## Origin

The term emerged on TikTok in the mid-2020s, combining the hormone "cortisol" with "coded" (a popular suffix on the platform). It reflects the generation's tendency to use scientific concepts in everyday slang.
