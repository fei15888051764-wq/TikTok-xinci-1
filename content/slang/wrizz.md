## Pronunciation
/rɪz/

## What Does "Wrizz" Mean?

**AI Overview Core Extraction:** "Wrizz" is a TikTok slang term for charisma, specifically the ability to attract romantic or social interest. A blend of "W" (win) and "rizz" (charisma), suggesting someone has winning charm.

## The Origins of "Wrizz"

"Wrizz" is a portmanteau of:
- **W** — win, victory, success
- **Rizz** — charisma, game, ability to attract others

The combination suggests not just charisma, but *successful* charisma—someone whose charm actually produces results.

## How "Wrizz" Became Popular

The term emerged as "rizz" became saturated on TikTok. Users wanted a way to distinguish between having charisma (rizz) and successfully using that charisma to win someone over (wrizz). It's the difference between potential and execution.
