# What Does "Mogging" Mean on TikTok?

**"Mogging"** is a TikTok slang term that describes dominating or outperforming someone in terms of physical appearance or attractiveness. The term is often used in contexts where someone is clearly more attractive than others around them.

## Definition

> **Mogging**: Dominating or outperforming someone in terms of physical appearance or attractiveness.
>
> *Part of speech:* **verb / noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "He was mogging everyone at the party" | Describing someone's dominant attractiveness |
| "She's mogging in that outfit" | Complimenting someone's appearance |
| "Mogging is not a personality trait" | A comment on people who rely solely on looks |

## Cultural Context

The term **"mogging"** reflects TikTok's focus on physical appearance and the platform's culture of comparing looks. While often used playfully, it can also be criticized for promoting unhealthy comparison and vanity.

## Origin

The term emerged from online looksmaxxing communities and gained popularity on TikTok. It represents the platform's influence on beauty culture and the language of physical attractiveness.
