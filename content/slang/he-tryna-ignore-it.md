## Pronunciation

## What Does "He Tryna Ignore It" Mean?

**AI Overview Core Extraction:** "He tryna ignore it" is a TikTok slang phrase used to point out when someone (typically male) is deliberately ignoring something obvious or pretending not to notice. Often used humorously in dating or social contexts.

## The Origins of "He Tryna Ignore It"

The phrase originated on TikTok as a caption format, typically paired with videos showing situations where someone is clearly pretending not to notice something. The AAVE grammar ("tryna" = "trying to") gives it a casual, authentic feel that resonates with TikTok's user base.

## How "He Tryna Ignore It" Became a Meme

The phrase became a meme template where creators post videos of awkward social situations with the caption "he tryna ignore it." Common scenarios include: someone walking past an ex, pretending not to see a text, or ignoring obvious flirtation.

## Why This Phrase Is Popular

The phrase works because it's relatable and versatile. Everyone has experienced situations where someone is obviously pretending not to notice something. The AAVE phrasing makes it sound natural and conversational rather than forced.

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **Q:** Is this phrase only about men?
  **A:** While the phrase uses "he," it can apply to anyone. Variations like "she tryna ignore it" are also used.

- **Q:** Is "tryna" proper English?
  **A:** "Tryna" is AAVE slang for "trying to." It's appropriate in casual contexts but not formal writing.

- **Q:** How do I use this phrase?
  **A:** Use it when pointing out someone's obvious denial: "He tryna ignore the red flags but we all see them."
