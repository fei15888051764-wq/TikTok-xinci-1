# What Does "He Tryna Ignore It" Mean on TikTok?

**"He Tryna Ignore It"** is a TikTok phrase used to describe someone who is clearly trying to avoid acknowledging something obvious or uncomfortable. The phrase is often used in reaction videos and comments.

## Definition

> **He Tryna Ignore It**: A phrase used to describe someone who is clearly trying to avoid acknowledging something obvious.
>
> *Part of speech:* **phrase**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "He tryna ignore it but we all see it" | Pointing out someone's obvious avoidance |
| "Look at him, he tryna ignore it" | A reaction to someone ignoring the obvious |
| "He tryna ignore it so hard" | Emphasizing someone's deliberate avoidance |

## Cultural Context

The phrase **"he tryna ignore it"** reflects TikTok's culture of observational humor and calling out obvious situations. It is often used in duets and reaction videos where users point out someone else's behavior.
