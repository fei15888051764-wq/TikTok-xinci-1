# What Does "Lore Dumping" Mean on TikTok?

**"Lore Dumping"** is a TikTok slang term that describes sharing a large amount of background information or "lore" about a topic, often in an enthusiastic or overwhelming way. The term is commonly used in fandom and gaming communities.

## Definition

> **Lore Dumping**: Sharing a large amount of background information or "lore" about a topic, often in an enthusiastic or overwhelming way.

The term **"lore dumping"** reflects TikTok's fandom and gaming cultures, where detailed background information about fictional worlds is highly valued. It describes the enthusiastic sharing of this information, which can sometimes be overwhelming for those not familiar with the topic.
