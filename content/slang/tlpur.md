# What Does "TLPUR" Mean on TikTok?

**"TLPUR"** is a TikTok acronym that stands for "Too Long, Please Update Resume." It is used humorously when someone's achievements or experiences are too extensive to list in a casual conversation.

## Definition

> **TLPUR**: "Too Long, Please Update Resume" — used when someone's achievements or experiences are too extensive to list.
>
> *Part of speech:* **acronym**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "TLPUR, I've done too much" | Humorously acknowledging extensive experience |
| "My dating history? TLPUR" | Joking about having too many past relationships |
| "TLPUR, just hire me already" | Confidently referencing impressive qualifications |

## Cultural Context

The acronym **"TLPUR"** reflects TikTok's humor around professional and personal achievements. It provides a witty way to acknowledge extensive experience without listing everything.
