## Pronunciation
/huː ɪz hiː/

## What Does "Who Is He" Mean?

**AI Overview Core Extraction:** "Who is he" is a TikTok phrase used to express surprise or admiration at someone's transformation, glow-up, or unexpected talent. Often used when someone reveals a side of themselves you didn't know existed.

## The Origins of "Who Is He"

The phrase started as a literal question but evolved into an exclamation of admiration. It's typically used in transition videos where someone shows a dramatic before-and-after, or when someone demonstrates a hidden talent.

## How "Who Is He" Became a Meme

The phrase became a meme through "glow-up" and "transformation" videos. The humor comes from the dramatic shift—someone goes from looking ordinary to extraordinary, and the comment "who is he" captures the disbelief.

## Real Usage in Native Context

> **TikTok Comment:** "He went from awkward teen to model in 2 years... who is he??"

> **Caption:** "POV: You haven't seen your friend since high school and he got hot #whoishe"

> **Duet:** "When the quiet kid at work reveals he can sing opera... WHO IS HE"

## Why "Who Is He" Resonates

The phrase captures the universal experience of being surprised by someone's hidden qualities. It's flattering when directed at you and entertaining when you use it on others.

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **Q:** Is "who is he" only for men?
  **A:** No, variations like "who is she" and "who are they" are also used.

- **Q:** Can it be used negatively?
  **A:** Rarely. It's almost always a compliment or expression of admiration.

- **Q:** How do I use it correctly?
  **A:** Use it when someone surprises you with their appearance, talent, or transformation: "You learned to play guitar in a month? Who is he??"
