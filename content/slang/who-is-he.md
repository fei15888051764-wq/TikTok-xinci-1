# What Does "Who Is He" Mean on TikTok?

**"Who Is He"** is a TikTok phrase used to express surprise or admiration at someone's transformation or unexpected behavior. It is often used in reaction to glow-up videos or unexpected talent reveals.

## Definition

> **Who Is He**: A phrase used to express surprise or admiration at someone's transformation or unexpected behavior.
>
> *Part of speech:* **phrase**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "Who is he?!" | Reacting to an impressive transformation |
| "Who is he with that new haircut" | Admiring someone's new look |
| "I saw him dance and thought, who is he" | Expressing surprise at hidden talent |

## Cultural Context

The phrase **"who is he"** reflects TikTok's culture of transformation content and the platform's love for surprising reveals. It is often used in duets and reaction videos.
