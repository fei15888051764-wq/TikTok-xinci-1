# What Does "Chopped" Mean on TikTok?

**"Chopped"** is a TikTok slang term used to describe being rejected, ignored, or dismissed, particularly in social or romantic contexts. The term is often used to describe situations where someone has been turned down or overlooked.

## Definition

> **Chopped**: Rejected, ignored, or dismissed, often in a social or romantic context.
>
> *Part of speech:* **verb / adjective**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "I got chopped at the party" | A person describing being ignored socially |
| "She chopped him hard" | Someone describing a harsh rejection |
| "Feeling chopped right now" | Expressing feelings of being dismissed |

## Cultural Context

The term **"chopped"** reflects the direct and sometimes harsh nature of social interactions on TikTok. It provides a concise way to describe rejection and has become part of the platform's vocabulary for discussing social dynamics.

## Related Slang

- **Ghosted** — When someone suddenly stops communicating with you
- **Left on Read** — When someone reads your message but doesn't respond
- **Curve** — To reject someone's romantic advances
- **Friend-Zoned** — When someone sees you only as a friend, not romantically

## Pronunciation

/tʃɒpt/

## Origin

The term emerged on TikTok in the mid-2020s as a way to describe rejection in social situations. Its origins may be linked to the idea of being "cut" or "chopped" from a group or situation.
