# What Does "Slopper" Mean on TikTok?

**"Slopper"** is a TikTok slang term used to describe someone who is messy, disorganized, or lacks hygiene. The term is often used as a mild insult or playful tease.

## Definition

> **Slopper**: Someone who is messy, disorganized, or lacks hygiene; a person who makes a mess of everything.
>
> *Part of speech:* **noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "You're such a slopper" | Playfully teasing someone for being messy |
| "My roommate is a slopper" | Complaining about a messy roommate |
| "Slopper behavior" | Commenting on someone's messy actions |

## Cultural Context

The term **"slopper"** reflects TikTok's culture of calling out behaviors in a humorous way. While it can be used as an insult, it is often used playfully among friends.

## Origin

The term emerged on TikTok as a way to describe messy behavior. Its origins may be linked to the word "slop," which refers to wet, messy food or liquid.
