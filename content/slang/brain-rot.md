# What Does "Brain Rot" Mean on TikTok?

**"Brain Rot"** is a TikTok slang term that describes the perceived deterioration of mental faculties from consuming too much low-quality online content. The term is often used humorously but also reflects genuine concerns about the impact of excessive screen time on cognitive function.

## Definition

> **Brain Rot**: The deterioration of mental faculties from consuming too much low-quality online content.
>
> *Part of speech:* **noun**

## Usage Examples

| Example | Context |
|---------|---------|
| "I have brain rot from TikTok" | A person joking about their excessive app usage |
| "This content gives me brain rot" | A comment on particularly vapid content |
| "Brain rot is real" | Acknowledging the genuine concern |

## Cultural Context

The term **"brain rot"** reflects Gen Z's self-aware humor about their own media consumption habits. While used playfully, it also speaks to a growing awareness of the potential negative effects of algorithm-driven content consumption on attention spans and cognitive abilities.

## Related Slang

- **Bed Rotting** — Spending excessive time in bed doing unproductive activities
- **Touch Grass** — A reminder to go outside and experience the real world
- **Goblin Mode** — Unapologetically prioritizing one's own comfort and desires

## Pronunciation

/breɪn rɒt/

## Origin

The term gained popularity on TikTok in the early-to-mid 2020s as users began discussing the effects of excessive screen time. It reflects the generation's unique position of being both heavy consumers of digital content and acutely aware of its potential downsides.
